# Archive Taxonomy

Use this reference when a conversation needs durable tags, strategic classification, or later retrieval.

## Value Buckets

- `战略判断`: direction, positioning, market choice, business model, competitive moat.
- `产品洞察`: user scenario, workflow pain, product surface, UX pattern, adoption blocker.
- `技术架构`: model choice, RAG, Agent architecture, knowledge graph, integration, infrastructure.
- `数据资产`: data source, data quality, ontology, feedback loop, proprietary dataset, governance.
- `组织协同`: ownership, operating rhythm, incentives, resource allocation, internal alignment.
- `市场验证`: customer signal, partner signal, investor feedback, competitor signal, industry constraint.
- `风险预警`: execution risk, compliance risk, dependency, cost issue, model limitation, trust issue.
- `待验证假设`: claim worth testing before product, technical, or commercial commitment.

## Importance Levels

- `高`: Affects strategic direction, core architecture, funding story, major product bets, or near-term decisions.
- `中`: Affects a workstream, feature area, partner path, or hypothesis that needs follow-up.
- `低`: Useful context, background signal, or weak evidence.

## Status Labels

- `待跟进`: Action or confirmation is needed.
- `已形成决策`: The conversation resulted in a clear decision.
- `需再次确认`: Source is useful but uncertain or incomplete.
- `仅作背景记录`: Preserve for context; no immediate action.
- `已进入验证`: A hypothesis from the conversation is now being tested.
